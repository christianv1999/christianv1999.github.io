Description:

The CrossClimate² is Michelin’s Grand Touring All-Season tire designed for sedans, SUVs, and other vehicles needing reliable performance in dry, wet, and light snow conditions. It meets severe snow service requirements and carries the three-peak mountain snowflake (3PMSF) symbol, making it ideal for year-round use without needing a dedicated winter tire. The tire features a Thermal Adaptive Tread Compound for flexibility in cold weather and a V-Formation tread design for improved grip in all conditions. PIANO Noise Reduction Tuning minimizes road noise, while V Ramp chamfers and 3D SipeLock Technology enhance braking, handling, and traction. Steep tread angles help resist hydroplaning, and the grooveless design improves wear and adds biting edges for winter performance. Internally, the CrossClimate² has a two-ply polyester casing, two steel belts, and a polyamide reinforcement for better stability at high speeds. Note: While tires with the 3PMSF symbol offer better snow traction than standard all-season (M+S) tires, they do not match the performance of true winter tires in extreme conditions.
